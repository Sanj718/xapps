## Developer Plan

If you're building or testing on a **Shopify development store**, you can request a free **Dev plan** that includes all Pro features. Contact us through the in-app support form to request access.

## Choosing the Right Plan

- **Free** — great for stores that need a single redirect to one other store or market
- **Basic** — ideal for stores with multiple regional domains that need full customization and tracking
- **Pro** — best for stores with complex requirements: page-specific targeting, custom JS logic, unlimited redirects, and Markets auto-redirects
